Quick Answers

Common Questions

How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days across India. Express (1–2 days) is available at checkout. Free shipping on orders over ₹2,999.
What's your return policy? +
We offer 30-day hassle-free returns on all unworn items with original tags. Just contact us and we'll arrange a free pickup. Refund processed within 5–7 business days.
How do I find my size? +
Check the size guide on each product page. Our fits run true to size. For compression fits, size up if you're between sizes. If you need help, email us with your measurements.
Do you ship internationally? +
Currently we ship across India. International shipping to UAE, UK, and Australia is launching in Q3 2025. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified.
How do I care for my Forged apparel? +
Machine wash cold, inside out, on gentle cycle. No fabric softener — it reduces moisture-wicking performance. Tumble dry low or air dry. Do not iron directly on printed areas.
Do you offer team / bulk discounts? +
Yes! Orders of 10+ units get 15% off. Orders of 25+ units get 25% off with custom labeling available. Contact our wholesale team at wholesale@forgedapparel.in.
